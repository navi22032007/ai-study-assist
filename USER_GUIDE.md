# StudyAI User Guide 🎓

Welcome to **StudyAI**, your premium AI-powered study companion. This guide will walk you through every feature and option available in the application.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Sign In
*   Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign In"** on the landing page.
*   Use your **Google Account** to authenticate. 
*   **Why?** This secures your documents and enables features like study streaks and personalized analytics.

### 2. Onboarding
*   On your first login, a **6-step tutorial** will appear. 
*   It highlights the Sidebar, Upload section, and Analytics. We highly recommend completing it!

---

## 📁 Managing Your Library

### Uploading Documents
*   Navigate to the **Upload** page.
*   You can drag and drop or click to select files.
*   **Supported Formats:** PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and TXT.
*   **Limit:** Up to 50MB per file.
*   **Pro Tip:** Organize files into **Folders** (e.g., "Physics", "Term 1") during upload for easier management.

### Document Library
*   View all your documents in the **Library** section.
*   **Search:** Use the search bar to find documents by name.
*   **Rename/Delete:** Click the three dots (⋮) on any document card to edit its title or remove it permanently.

---

## 🧠 The Study View (Analysis)

When you click on a document, you enter the **Study View**. This is where the AI power happens!

### 1. AI Summary & ELI5
*   **AI Summary:** Generates a concise summary (under 200 words).
*   **ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5):** Simplifies complex concepts into language even a child would understand.
*   **Translation:** Seamlessly translate the summary into 10+ languages (Spanish, French, Hindi, etc.).

### 2. Key Points & Bookmarks
*   AI extracts the most important concepts and labels them as **High**, **Medium**, or **Low** importance.
*   **Bookmarks:** Click the ribbon icon next to a key point to save it for later review.

### 3. Flashcards
*   Automatically generates interactive flashcards.
*   Click a card to flip it and reveal the answer.
*   Use the navigation arrows to flip through the deck.

### 4. Interactive Mind Map
*   Generates a visual diagram of the document's structure.
*   You can drag nodes, zoom in/out, and see how topics interconnect.

### 5. AI Vision (Diagram Analysis)
*   **Only for PDFs:** AI scans the document for charts, graphs, and diagrams.
*   It provides a detailed analysis of what each visual element represents.

### 6. Grounded Chat
*   Ask specific questions like *"What are the advantages of Newton's third law mentioned in this text?"*
*   The AI is **grounded**, meaning it will only answer based on your document's content, preventing "hallucinations."

## ✍️ Testing Your Knowledge

### Quiz Engine
*   Generate quizzes with 5, 10, or 15 questions.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, or Hard.
*   **Types:** MCQ, True/False, and Fill-in-the-blanks.
*   **Timer:** A countdown starts as soon as you begin. Don't let it run out!

### Achievements & Certificates
*   **Certificates:** If you score **80% or higher**, you are automatically awarded a verified certificate.
*   **Public Verification:** Each certificate has a unique QR code. Employers or teachers can scan it to verify your achievement.
*   **Download:** You can download your certificates as high-quality PDFs.

---

## 👥 Live Study Rooms (Collaborative Mode)

*   **Create a Room:** Generate a unique link and share it with friends.
*   **Mind Map Mode:** Everyone in the room sees the mind map update in real-time as the host expands it.
*   **Quiz Battle:** The host launches questions, and everyone competes! See who's at the top of the **Live Leaderboard**.
*   **Discussion:** Chat with your peers. You can even "anchor" messages to specific parts of the document.

---

## 📊 Analytics & Profile

### Study Dashboard
*   **Score History:** Track your quiz progress over time.
*   **Weak Topics:** The AI automatically identifies topics where you struggle (below 60%) and suggests you review them.
*   **Topic Performance:** A data visualization of which subjects you've mastered.

### Study Streak
*   Maintain a daily streak by logging in and studying. Don't break the chain!

### Focus Mode (Experimental)
*   Enters full-screen mode to minimize distractions.
*   **XP Penalty:** Be careful! Switching tabs or minimizing the window during focus mode will deduct 30 XP from your profile.

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
*   **Data Isolation:** Your documents are strictly your own. No other user can access your files unless you generate a **Share Link**.
*   **Share Links:** Shared links expire automatically after **7 days** for your privacy.
*   **Encrypted Handshakes:** All real-time room communication is protected by JWT authentication.
